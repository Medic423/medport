### Completed Trips Tab

#### Historical Completed Trips, Reporting & Analysis
When a trip has been **Marked Completed** in the **In-Progress** screen the trip is automatically advanced to the **Completed Trips** list for reference, analysis and export. 

#### Key Features
**Completed Trip List**
  - All trips with COMPLETED status
  - Date-based organization
**Trip Details**
  - Completion time
- **Time Calculations**
  - Acceptance Time: Time from trip dispatch to acceptance from EMS or other transport provider
  - Wait Time: Time from EMS acceptance until the transport provide arrives at the pick-up location
- **Export Options**
  - Export to CSV
  - Print trip details
