# TRACC Quick Start Guide

**Welcome to TRACC!** This guide will help you get started with creating your account and logging in.

---

## Understanding Account Types

TRACC offers two types of accounts, each designed for different users:

### Healthcare Facility Account

**Choose this account type if you are:**
- A hospital or health system
- An urgent care clinic
- A nursing home or skilled nursing facility
- A rehabilitation facility
- A dialysis center
- An assisted living facility
- Any healthcare facility that needs to arrange patient transportation

**What Healthcare accounts can do:**
- Create and submit transport requests
- View available EMS providers
- Track transport status in real-time
- Manage multiple facility locations (if applicable)
- Coordinate patient transfers efficiently

### EMS Agency Account

**Choose this account type if you are:**
- An Emergency Medical Services (EMS) provider
- A wheelchair van operator
- A non-emergency medical transport company
- A critical care transport service
- An air ambulance service
- Any private transportation company that wants to receive alerts about transport requests from healthcare facilities

**What EMS accounts can do:**
- Receive notifications about new transport requests
- View available trips in your service area
- Accept or decline transport requests
- Manage your agency information and capabilities
- Track completed transports

## Information Checklist

Before you start filling out the registration form, gather the following information:

### For Healthcare Facilities

**Required Information:**
- **Facility Name** - The official name of your healthcare facility
- **Facility Type** - Select from: Hospital, Urgent Care, Clinic, Dialysis Center, Nursing Home, Assisted Living, or Other
- **Contact Person Name** - Your name or the primary contact person
- **Email Address** - A valid email address for your account (you'll use this to log in)
- **Password** - Choose a secure password
- **Phone Number** - Facility contact phone number
- **Street Address** - Complete street address of your facility
- **City** - City where your facility is located
- **State** - Two-letter state abbreviation (e.g., PA, NY, CA)
- **ZIP Code** - Five-digit ZIP code

**Optional but Recommended:**
- **GPS Coordinates** (Latitude/Longitude) - The system can automatically look these up from your address, but you can also enter them manually for more accurate location matching

**Additional Options:**
- **Manage Multiple Locations** - Check this box if you manage multiple hospitals, clinics, or facilities that need to arrange transportation

### For EMS Agencies

**Required Information:**
- **Agency Name** - The official name of your EMS agency or transport company
- **Agency Type** - Select from: Emergency Medical Services, Non-Emergency Medical Transport, Wheelchair Transport, Critical Care Transport, Neonatal Transport, Pediatric Transport, Bariatric Transport, Air Ambulance, Ground Ambulance, or Specialized Medical Transport
- **Contact Person Name** - Your name or the primary contact person
- **Email Address** - A valid email address for your account (you'll use this to log in)
- **Password** - Choose a secure password
- **Phone Number** - Agency contact phone number
- **Street Address** - Complete street address of your agency headquarters or main location
- **City** - City where your agency is located
- **State** - Two-letter state abbreviation (e.g., PA, NY, CA)
- **ZIP Code** - Five-digit ZIP code

**Optional but Recommended:**
- **GPS Coordinates** (Latitude/Longitude) - The system can automatically look these up from your address, but you can also enter them manually for more accurate location matching
- **Service Area** - Select the states or regions where you provide service
- **Capabilities** - Select your service capabilities: BLS, ALS, CCT, Critical Care, Neonatal, Pediatric, Bariatric, Isolation
- **Operating Hours** - Your agency's operating hours (defaults to 24/7 if not specified)

## Step-by-Step: Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Navigate to the Registration Page

1. Go to the TRACC website homepage
2. Click the **"Create Account"** or **"Get Started"** button
   - This button appears in multiple places:
     - Top navigation bar (right side)
     - Hero section
     - "Ready to Get Started?" section

### Step 2: Choose Your Account Type

After clicking "Create Account," you'll see a modal with two options:

1. **Healthcare Facility Account** (green button with building icon)
   - Click this if you're registering a healthcare facility

2. **EMS Agency Account** (orange button with truck icon)
   - Click this if you're registering an EMS or transport provider

**Important:** Choose the correct account type. If you're unsure, refer back to the "Understanding Account Types" section above.

### Step 3: Fill Out the Registration Form

1. **Enter all required information** from your checklist above
2. **For Healthcare accounts:**
   - Select your facility type from the dropdown
   - If you manage multiple locations, check the "Manage Multiple Locations" box
   - The system will automatically look up GPS coordinates from your address (you'll see a "Look up GPS Coordinates" button)

3. **For EMS accounts:**
   - Select your agency type from the dropdown
   - Optionally select your service area and capabilities
   - Optionally set your operating hours
   - The system will automatically look up GPS coordinates from your address (you'll see a "Look up GPS Coordinates" button)

4. **Review your information** to ensure everything is correct

5. **Click "Create Account"** or "Submit" button

### Step 4: Account Creation Confirmation

After submitting the form:

- **Healthcare accounts:** You'll see a success message confirming your account has been created. Your account will be active immediately for a free trial period.
- **EMS accounts:** You'll see a success message confirming your account has been created. Your account will be active immediately.

**Note:** If GPS coordinates couldn't be automatically determined, you can add them later in your account settings. The system will show you a reminder, but you can still use your account.

## Logging Into Your Account

Once your account is created, you can log in immediately:

### Step 1: Navigate to the Login Page

1. Go to the TRACC website homepage
2. Click the **"Login"** button in the top navigation bar
   - Or use the login form in the hero section of the homepage

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

1. **Email Address:** Enter the email address you used during registration
2. **Password:** Enter the password you created during registration
3. **Remember Me (optional):** Check this box if you want to stay logged in on this device

### Step 3: Click "Login"

Click the **"Login"** button to access your account.

### Step 4: Access Your Dashboard

After successful login:
- **Healthcare accounts:** You'll be taken to your Healthcare Dashboard where you can create transport requests
- **EMS accounts:** You'll be taken to your EMS Dashboard where you can view available trips and manage your agency

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Remember Your Password?

Click the **"Forgot password?"** link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

### Account Already Exists Error

If you see an error saying an account with your email already exists:
- Try logging in with that email address instead
- If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot password?" link
- If you believe this is an error, contact support

### GPS Coordinates Not Found

If the system can't automatically find GPS coordinates for your address:
- You can still create your account
- Add GPS coordinates later in your account settings:
  - **Healthcare:** Go to Facility Settings
  - **EMS:** Go to Agency Info

### Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during registration or login:
- Check that all required fields are filled out
- Ensure your email address is valid and correctly formatted
- Make sure your password meets the requirements (if any)
- Contact TRACC support for assistance

## Account Status and Free Trial

**Important:** All new accounts start with a free trial period. Your account will be active immediately upon creation and remain active for one week. After the free trial period, you'll need to sign up for a paid account to continue using TRACC services.

**Note:** The system is currently being prepared to support converting free accounts to paid accounts. More information about pricing and account conversion will be available soon.

## Next Steps After Creating Your Account

### For Healthcare Facilities:

1. **Complete your facility profile** - Add any additional information in Facility Settings
2. **Add GPS coordinates** (if not automatically set) - This helps match you with nearby EMS providers
3. **Add additional locations** (if applicable) - If you manage multiple facilities, add them in Settings
4. **Create your first transport request** - Start using TRACC to coordinate patient transfers

### For EMS Agencies:

1. **Complete your agency profile** - Add service capabilities and operating hours in Agency Info
2. **Add GPS coordinates** (if not automatically set) - This helps match you with nearby transport requests
3. **Set your service area** - Define the regions where you provide service
4. **Start receiving trip notifications** - You'll be notified when transport requests match your capabilities and location
